Multiple disease predictor site

This app is designed to help you predict the likelihood of various diseases using advanced machine learning models. Our goal is to provide accurate and timely predictions for critical conditions such as:

  • Heart Disease – Evaluate your risk based on common health indicators like cholesterol levels, blood pressure, and more.
  • Diabetes – Estimate your likelihood of developing diabetes by analyzing key parameters like glucose levels and BMI.
  • Skin disease detection – By uploading a picture of the affected skin area, this app attempts to predict the type of skin disease and provides a confidence score for the prediction. However, this app is currently limited to predicting only 6 classes of skin diseases, namely: Benign Keratosis, Melanoma, Melanotic Cell, Basal Cell Carcinoma, Eczema, and Actinic Keratosis.
